### Q1

The mean of two of these numbers is *q*.
The mean of the remaining numbers is 10.
Which of the following is a correct expression for *n* in terms of *p* and *q*?

- (A) 2(*q* − 10) / (*p* − 10)
- (B) 2(*q* − 10) / (10 − *p*)
- (C) 2(*q* − 10) / (*p* + 10)
- (D) 2(10 − *q*) / (*p* + 10)
- (E) 2(10 + *q*) / (*p* − 10)
- (F) 2(10 + *q*) / (10 − *p*)
